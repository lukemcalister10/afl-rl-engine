exactly the certain years. Measured at the anchors (level → premium, share of above-replacement margin):

or not); "permanent captain, reliable high floor+ceiling for the captain slot" is a slot/role property the model
cannot see. **PROPOSAL (owner rules, NOT implemented):** an owner-maintained captains/VC register (like
LTI_REGISTER / OWNER_OVERRIDES O1) feeding a small explicit present-tense captain-slot bonus, visible as an
owner-dial layer. Until ruled, the production-keyed premium is the modelled captaincy and it is fully credited
by this candidate.
