**Bias direction (measured):** a per-band mean of the yr1 anchor UNDERSTATES the pick — `peak/anchor` > 1 in every band. The ABSOLUTE gap (peak-anchor) is largest at the TOP (~1956 SCAR at picks 1-3 vs ~890 at 36-48); the RATIO is U-shaped, smallest at picks 1-3 (1.69 — top picks play early, so their yr1 anchor is already high) and largest MID-BAND (~2.6 at 28-48). So the NOTE's 'worst at the top' holds in **dollars**, not in ratio. A flat yr1 snapshot credits none of the tail; the MEDIAN smoother flattens what little the anchor carries.
